Gardening is just not a hobby for me — it’s a deep-rooted passion that continues to shape how I live, breathe, and think.

The act of nurturing a plant — watering it, pruning it, protecting it from pests — connects me deeply with nature and myself.
Gardening isn’t just something I do; it’s become a part of who I am. It reminds me every day that beauty takes time, growth requires care, and sometimes, the best things in life are grown slowly.
